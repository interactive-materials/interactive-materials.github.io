Interactive Materials Lab
Projects People

We research material systems to support interaction and experience design.

Our practice blends designerly exploration and technical experiments with rigorous making. We specialize in building interactive systems by leveraging everyday materials and democratic making processes; this often yields new approaches and tools for constructing tangible interfaces.

The Interactive Materials Lab is housed within the Division of Industrial Design at the National University of Singapore.
